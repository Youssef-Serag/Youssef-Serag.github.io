Intro

Hello, I'm Youssef Elsayed Serag, a dedicated and multifaceted professional with a strong foundation in both hardware and software engineering. My journey has taken me through an impressive array of internships and roles, including stints at industry giants such as Intel, Texas Instruments, OnSemi, and Fluke Corporation in the hardware domain, and Garmin, American Express, and Atlassian in the software arena. These experiences have equipped me with a broad skill set, allowing me to seamlessly navigate and contribute to diverse technological environments.

In addition to my hands-on technical experience, I have a robust academic background, having served as a teaching assistant to over 500 students and participated in research under esteemed professors. My passion for mentorship extends beyond the classroom, as I actively guide individuals in their pursuit of internships, resume building, and career transitions. Currently, I am leveraging my expertise to develop a custom application on Contentful, demonstrating my continuous drive to innovate and solve complex problems. Welcome to my website, where you can explore my projects, insights, and the unique blend of skills I bring to the tech industry.

Work

Hardware Engineering Internships

Intel Corporation

During my time at Intel, I had the opportunity to work on cutting-edge semiconductor technologies. I was involved in designing and testing hardware components, contributing to the development of high-performance processors. My work included collaborating with cross-functional teams to enhance product reliability and efficiency, conducting detailed performance analyses, and troubleshooting complex hardware issues. This experience honed my skills in circuit design, signal processing, and hardware validation, laying a strong foundation for my career in hardware engineering.

Texas Instruments

At Texas Instruments, I focused on analog and embedded systems, where I contributed to the development of advanced microcontrollers and integrated circuits. My role involved designing and simulating circuit models, optimizing power consumption, and ensuring the robustness of embedded systems. I also worked on improving the scalability of production processes, which provided me with a comprehensive understanding of manufacturing challenges and solutions in the semiconductor industry.

OnSemi

My internship at OnSemi allowed me to delve into power management and semiconductor solutions. I worked on developing efficient power converters and controllers, which are critical for various electronic applications. This role required me to engage in detailed circuit analysis, PCB layout design, and testing procedures to ensure the highest standards of quality and performance. The hands-on experience I gained here was invaluable in deepening my expertise in power electronics and system-level design.

Fluke Corporation

At Fluke Corporation, I was part of the team that designs precision measurement instruments. My responsibilities included developing and testing hardware prototypes, performing rigorous validation tests, and ensuring compliance with industry standards. I also contributed to the improvement of product design processes by implementing innovative solutions to reduce production costs and enhance product reliability. This internship strengthened my analytical and problem-solving skills, particularly in the context of hardware diagnostics and calibration.

Software Engineering Internships

Garmins

During my internship at Garmin, I worked on software development for navigation and wearable technology. I was involved in coding, testing, and debugging software applications that enhance user experiences for GPS devices and smartwatches. My contributions included developing new features, optimizing existing code, and collaborating with cross-functional teams to integrate software with hardware components. This experience allowed me to apply my programming skills in C++ and Python, and gain insights into the complexities of embedded software development.

American Express

At American Express, I contributed to the development of secure and scalable financial software solutions. My role involved back-end development, database management, and implementing robust security measures to protect sensitive financial data. I worked closely with senior developers to design and optimize algorithms that improve transaction processing efficiency. This internship provided me with a deeper understanding of financial technologies and the importance of cybersecurity in the software development lifecycle.

Atlassian

My time at Atlassian was spent working on software tools that enhance team collaboration and productivity. I contributed to the development of features for Jira and Confluence, focusing on improving user interfaces and backend functionality. My responsibilities included writing clean, maintainable code, conducting code reviews, and participating in agile development practices. This experience highlighted the importance of user-centered design and the power of collaboration tools in fostering efficient workflows in modern workplaces.
